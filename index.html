A collection of sexist stories told by capitalism

Concept: Records of stories, told by sexist advertising from our database. Each poster will be described in a story, without showing the poster itself. These stories are available as audio files in a website.

When printed, the stories appear in a book, printed as a collection of stories that show the danger of the narratives. Maybe also the posters appear in an abstract way (e.g. collages).
